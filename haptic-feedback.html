HCI Research Project - Haptic Feedback Device

Back

Course: CS347: Human Computer Interaction: Foundations and Frontiers

Timeline: 4 weeks

Skills: User Research, Arduino, CAD Modeling (Fusion 360)

About: Our study explores the efficacy of haptic notifications as a means to promote healthier mobile phone usage habits. The project involved developing a haptic feedback device that employed physical vibrations to alert users to screen time limits. We conducted two experimental studies.

I designed and manufactured the device prototype, recruited and onboarded users, and contributed to authoring the research paper.
